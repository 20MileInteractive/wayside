Rooms & Amenities at Chatham Wayside Inn

The spacious Inn has 56 guest rooms, each with private baths (some with whirlpool spa jets), flat screen TVs, DVD players, air-conditioning and heat, along with helpful touches such as hair dryers and irons. Some rooms have porches or patios with lovely views of the adjacent park or the Chatham Seaside Links Golf Course. There are rooms with fireplaces, and many feature four-poster beds.

Dining

The Wild Goose Tavern is our hotel restaurant, available for breakfast lunch and dinner 7 days a week. The Goose features an extensive wine and cocktail menu, and is also available for room service to our guests.

Pool

Enjoy our outdoor heated pool Memorial Day through Columbus Day.

Children

The Chatham Wayside Inn welcomes children of all ages.

Pets

Please make other arrangements for your pets. Though we love pets, they are not allowed at the Inn.

Special Events

We have the perfect location for any special event, from a wedding party to corporate retreat. Please contact our events coordinator to learn more.
